Physical Weapons

Sea Halberd

Life Drain: Upon dealing damage to the target, reduces Shield and HP Regen of the target by 50% for 3s.

+70 Physical ATK; +25% Attack Speed

Blade Of Despair

Despair: Attacking enemy units that have HP below 50% will increase hero's Physical Attack by 25%. Lasts 2s. (Takes affect before damage is dealt)

+160 Physical ATK; +5% Movement SPD

Demon Hunter Sword

Devour: Basic attacks will deal 9% of the target's current HP as additional physical damage (up to 60 against creeps and minions). Devour: Each Basic Attack grants 3% Physical Lifesteal for 3s. Stack up to 5 times.

+35 Physical ATK; +25% Attack Speed

Blade of the Heptaseas

+15 Physical PEN Ambush: If no damage is taken or dealt within 5s, the next Basic Attack will deal extra Physical Damage equal to 160 plus 40% Physical Attack, and inflict 40% slow effects upon the target for 1.5s.

+250 HP +70 Physical ATK

Magic Weapons



Lightning Truncheon

Resonate: Every 6s, the next skill echoes, dealing 20-1000 Magic Damage (scales with the hero's Max Mana) to up to 3 enemies

+75 Magic Power; +300 Mana; +10% CD Reduction

Blood Wings

Guard: Gain a Shield equal to 200% Magic Power. Gain another after 30s when it breaks.

+500 HP; +150 Magic Power

Glowing Wand

Scorch: Skill Damage will burn the targets for 3s, dealing 1% of the target's Max HP as Magic Damage per second (damaging the same target multiple times within a short duration can increase this damage to 2% at most), 10 damage minimum

+400 HP +75 Magic Power

Shadow Twinblades

Assassination: If no damage is taken or dealt within 5s, the next Basic Attack will deal extra Magic Damage equal to 200 plus 50% Magic Power, and inflict 60% slow effects upon the target for 1.5s.

+75 Magic Power; +15% Magic Lifesteal; +5% Movement SPD

Defense Items



Dominance Ice


+10% CD Reduction; Arctic Cold: Reduces the Shield and HP Regen of nearby enemy heroes by 50% (excluding those affected by "Life Drain"), and their Attack Speed by 30%.

+70 Physical Defense; +500 Mana; +5% Movement SPD

Athena's Shield

Shield: Can be triggered when taking Magic Damage. Reduces Magic Damage taken by 25% for 5s (including the Magic Damage that triggers this effect). You will be able to trigger this effect again only after leaving combat for 10s.

+900 HP; +2 HP Regen; +62 Magic Defense

Queen's Wings

Demonize: Reduces damage taken by 20% when health is less than 40% and increases your Spell Vamp by 35% for 5s. This effect has a cooldown of 60s.

+900 HP; +15 Physical ATK; +10% CD Reduction

Oracle

Bless: Raise shield absorption and HP regen effect by 30%.

+850 HP; +42 Magic Defense; +10% CD Reduction
